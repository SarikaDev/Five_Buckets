Summary :
The trigger is maintaining complete data integrity across your entire vault system:

🧹 Cleans up child records (withdrawal_sources)
🧹 Removes financial ledger entries (ledger_entries with SPEND)
🧹 Deletes the box event records (both WITHDRAW and SEALED types)
🔓 Unseals the bucket since the withdrawal is gone

So when someone deletes a withdrawal, the trigger ensures no orphaned data remains anywhere in the system - it's like a domino effect of cleanup!
